OKALEIGH TENNIS CLUB

Okaleigh Tennis Club

Founded as the first private equity tennis club of Oakleigh in 1879, OTC became the home courts for many players and competitions. Oakleigh’s players were among the first to compete when pennant competition commenced. Over the years, OTC has become a dynamic sporting and social nexus for our membership — a lively mix of juniors, seniors, singles, couples and families. Our historic facility offers a wide array of first-class athletic and social amenities. Its 16 courts remain a vibrant center of tennis, including two Har-Tru courts. OTC offers a full-service Pro Shop, first-rate Fitness & Aquatics facilities, a restaurant, and a bar where you can share a meal or some drinks with family and friends.
